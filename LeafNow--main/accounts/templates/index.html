Some common questions
were often asked

My flowers are falling off or dying?

Houseplants drop leaves for many reasons, but most are related to improper care or poor growing conditions. Often just giving plants the correct light and temperature, or controlling pests, is all that is needed to prevent future leaf drop. Either too much or too little watering may cause leaf drop

What causes leaves to become pale?

If the entire leaf is yellow or pale, but is staying attached to the stem, it may not be a nutrient problem, but a drainage problem. Roots sitting in a muddy hole cannot use water. When roots stop pulling up water, the whole plant suffers.

What causes brown crispy leaves?

Under-Watering. When your plant does not receive enough natural water (under-watering), it will start to turn brown and crispy

How do i choose a plant?

Choosing Plants Based on Garden Conditions Work with the space you have available. Plant large and fast-growing specimens if you have lots of space. Try container gardening if ground space is limited. Select plants that are suited to the soil at your growing site. Observe the light levels in your garden.

How do I change the pots?

The first step is to collect the required materials which include fresh or revitalized potting soil, Place some stones at the base of the new pot. This will help prevent the potting soil from flowing out of the drainage
